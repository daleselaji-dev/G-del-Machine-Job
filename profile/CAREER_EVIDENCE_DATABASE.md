## 1. Maintenance rules

- Append new evidence; do not delete earlier factual records.
- Fix spelling, grammar and formatting in place.
- For material corrections, add a new item with `SUPERSEDES` and retain the previous version in the change log.
- Better wording may become preferred wording; keep materially different useful alternatives.
- Resume-generated claims are not facts until user-confirmed or source-backed.
- Keep sensitive or confidential details outside this public repository.

| Status | Meaning | Resume use |
|---|---|---|
| `CONFIRMED` | Existing archive or explicit confirmation supports it. | Direct use allowed. |
| `IMPORTED_PENDING` | Appears in uploaded resumes but is not confirmed. | Confirm first. |
| `CONFLICT` | Sources or chronology disagree. | Do not use yet. |
| `DERIVED` | Interpretation based on confirmed facts. | Positioning only. |
| `EXCLUDED` | Overstated or unverifiable. | Do not use. |
