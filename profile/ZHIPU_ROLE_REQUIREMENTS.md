## 2.2 What the role actually owns

This is not a narrow social-media role. It combines five jobs:

1. **International product operations** — own the overseas operating plan rather than executing isolated campaigns.
2. **Growth operations** — acquire, activate and retain users and connect operating data to product decisions.
3. **Content and campaign operations** — build a content matrix and run integrated online/offline campaigns.
4. **Localization and community** — localize UI, documentation and marketing materials and maintain core users.
5. **Academic ecosystem development** — build relationships with universities, research institutes, conferences, forums and partners.

The role is therefore a hybrid of product operations, growth, international marketing, community and institutional partnerships.
