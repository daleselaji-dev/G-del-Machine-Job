## Retrieval checklist

For every target role:

1. extract the JD's top five responsibilities;
2. search this index for matching terms and adjacent evidence;
3. open the exact evidence IDs;
4. distinguish direct evidence from transferable/derived evidence;
5. identify missing proof;
6. generate the target resume from selected evidence without modifying the archive;
7. add newly verified experience, project or interview evidence to the append-only log.
