# DD Presentation Standard

Status: reference v1

Company research should have two layers:

1. Full research notes for evidence coverage.
2. A compressed presentation for decision-making.

## One-page dashboard

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Company / role / city | What is being evaluated |
| Industry | Main field and value chain |
| AI adjacency | Whether the role connects to AI or AI-driven demand |
| Product sellability | Whether the product has credible customer value |
| Company condition | Development, finance, governance and risk summary |
| Overseas reality | Export, customers, channels, certification and localization |
| Employer quality | Compensation, work system, team and manager risk |
| Candidate ecology | What the candidate enters and accumulates |
| Largest unknown | The most important unresolved issue |
| Rating | P0, P1, P2, WATCH or REJECT |
| Next action | Interview question, deeper research or stop |

## Detailed report sections

1. Temporary conclusion.
2. Evidence status and largest unknowns.
3. Company development and operating condition.
4. Industry and technology trend.
5. Product competitiveness and sellability.
6. Customers, channels and overseas business.
7. Employer and role quality.
8. Candidate entry ecology.
9. Interview validation questions.
10. Final rating and next action.

## Compression rule

If a detail does not change the rating, the largest unknown, the interview validation plan or the candidate's ecology fit, keep it out of the dashboard and put it in notes or appendix.
