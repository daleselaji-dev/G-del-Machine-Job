# Scorecard and Stage Gates

Status: reference v1

## Ratings

| Rating | Meaning |
|---|---|
| P0 | immediate priority if current role, company outlook, role fit and employer quality all pass |
| P1 | worth pursuing with one or two important unknowns |
| P2 | useful option or stretch route, not primary |
| WATCH | promising but not actionable or insufficiently verified |
| REJECT | fails constraints or lacks career value |

## Hard gates

| Gate | Question |
|---|---|
| Role reality | Is the role current and specific enough? |
| Location | Is the role in the preferred region or justified by high value? |
| Compensation | Is fixed pay likely near the target range or justified by exceptional path value? |
| Work system | Is there no visible long-term high-intensity or single-rest-day conflict? |
| Product reality | Is there real product, customer, deployment, revenue or channel evidence? |
| Company outlook | Is there evidence of demand, product competitiveness, business quality, customer growth or internationalization? |
| Career asset | Will the role build customer, product, technical, channel, solution or industry assets? |
| Evidence | Are key conclusions traceable to sources or marked unknown? |

## Stage gates

### L0 to L1

A company may move from L0 to L1 when it has a plausible industry, product, location and role fit.

### L1 to L2

A company may move from L1 to L2 when it could affect interview, application or offer decisions.

### L2 to interview validation

A company may move from L2 to interview validation when the largest unknowns can be directly tested through interview questions.

## Evidence labels

Use:

- Fact;
- Inference;
- Unknown;
- Insufficient evidence.

## Downgrade examples

| Signal | Typical action |
|---|---|
| role not current | WATCH or archive |
| product only appears in marketing | WATCH |
| employer risk is visible and unresolved | P2/WATCH/REJECT depending on severity |
| compensation or work system conflicts with constraints | REJECT unless user explicitly changes constraint |
| no career asset after role analysis | REJECT or archive |
