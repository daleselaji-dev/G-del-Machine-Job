# Project Manifest

## Mission

Build a resumable career agent that improves the user's probability of entering a high-quality company while preserving long-term career value, evidence quality, and user control.

## Current objective

- Search window: rolling 60-day interview campaign.
- Primary outcomes: qualified first-round interviews, at least one second-round interview, and a stronger bridge into enterprise AI / large-model commercialization.
- Current priority routes:
  1. DeepSeek product operations, cross-domain talent, Agent/data-product roles.
  2. Z.ai / 智谱 MaaS solutions, industry BD, ecosystem operations, large-model sales.
  3. Enterprise AI/data/cloud, overseas sales, multinational commercial, customer success, solutions, partner and trainee roles with strong prospects.

## Candidate profile snapshot

- 2+ years of technical-product overseas business development; nearly 3 years of international-market-related experience.
- Markets: Middle East, Latin America, the Americas, Europe and South Asia.
- Strengths: 0→1 market development, channel recruitment, exclusive-distributor negotiation, technical-product translation, OSINT/customs-data research, account segmentation, order/implementation coordination, customer adoption and sell-out support.
- Evidence: RMB 4M annual orders, 30 new channels, 13 ordering customers, 20% regional growth, 30% sell-out improvement, shortened deal/project cycles.
- Transition assets: bilingual communication, technical learning speed, AI tools/Agent practice, customer-problem discovery, cross-functional execution.
- Main gaps for AI roles: no sufficiently visible Agent portfolio, limited formal product-management artifacts, limited model evaluation and MaaS/API commercial vocabulary.

## Canonical files

- `SKILL.md`: executable runtime policy.
- `docs/CONSTITUTION.md`: protected goals and hard constraints.
- `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md`: control objects and information flow.
- `docs/FIELD_ONBOARDING_MAP.md`: evolving career-field map.
- `docs/EVOLUTION_PROTOCOL.md`: bounded self-improvement protocol.
- `docs/EVIDENCE_AND_DD.md`: job/company/product evidence rules.
- `evals/EVALUATION_TASKS.md`: frozen regression cases.
- `memory/STATE.md`: current operating state.
- `memory/PREFERENCES_AND_COMPANY_LEDGER.md`: stable preferences and company-specific state.
- `memory/RUN_SUMMARY.md`: rolling compressed run history.
- `memory/ROLLBACK.md`: active and rollback versions.

## Update discipline

- Daily runs update only runtime memory and current evidence-derived actions.
- Company-specific facts stay in the ledger, not in the global policy.
- Detailed one-off research is summarized; raw conversational history is not copied into the repository.
- Any policy change must be proposed on a dedicated branch/PR and pass the frozen evaluation set.
